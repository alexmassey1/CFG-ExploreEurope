This website has been produced as part of the CodeFirst: Girls beginners course. Over 8 weeks we have had an introduction into the world of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery and GitHub. With this new coding knowledge we have been able to create the 'Explore Europe' website to express our love of travelling within Europe. Each member of the team has travelled to a variety of different places in Europe, therefore we wanted to collaborate our passion for exploring new places with our newly found love of coding.
